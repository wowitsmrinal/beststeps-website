Curious about Ambi?

Is Ambi really free?

Yes. No ads, no subscriptions, no data collection. Just the sounds.

Can I use Ambi without internet?

Yep. Once you've downloaded the app, all sounds are stored on your device so it keeps working offline—great for flights or low-signal places.

Does Ambi drain battery?

Not much. It's built for long, quiet hours. If you're sleeping, just lock the screen and let it play.

Does it work on iPad or Mac?

For now, it's iPhone-only. A wider version may come once it feels right.

Who made Ambi?

Just one person who likes calm things and simple design. I built Ambi for myself first, then shared it because others seemed to need it too.
